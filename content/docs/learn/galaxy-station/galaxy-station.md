Galaxy Station by Hexxagon offers a WalletConnect-capable browser extension for Terra Classic. Use this section to install the wallet, manage accounts, and perform common actions across mainnet (`columbus-5`) and the `rebel-2` testnet.

## Before using Galaxy Station

- Confirm Terra Classic mainnet is selected before mainnet transactions.
- Confirm your account address starts with `terra`.
- Keep seed phrases offline and never enter them into websites.
- Use a small test transaction before sending meaningful value.
- Keep enough LUNC liquid for fees.
- Verify exchange memo requirements before deposits.
- Treat WalletConnect prompts as transaction prompts, not as automatic trust.

## Safety baseline

Galaxy Station should ask you to connect, approve, or sign. It should not ask you to reveal your recovery phrase during normal website use.

If a website asks for your seed phrase, close it.
